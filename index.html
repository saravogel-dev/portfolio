About

The research
behind the design.

I'm an ethnomusicologist and qualitative researcher who teaches undergraduate courses at the University of Florida. My dissertation examines feminist embodiment and sonic agency in the Iranian Woman, Life, Freedom movement.

I transitioned into UX research because the skills are the same: deep listening, pattern recognition, and translating what people say into what they actually need. I bring a PhD researcher's rigor to every study — and a designer's eye to every solution.

I hold a WCAG 2.2 accessibility certification, completed the Google UX Design Certificate, and have a background in industrial engineering. I'm fluent in English and Farsi.
